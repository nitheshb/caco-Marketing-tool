To connect your Instagram account to Agent Elephant, you need to set up a developer application within the Meta (Facebook) developer portal to generate an **App ID** and an **App Secret**.

This guide walks you through the exact steps needed in Meta's latest developer dashboard layout.

---

## Part 1: Setting up the Meta Application

Both Instagram Professional accounts and Creator accounts require a Meta Developer App to allow third-party posting.

### Step 1: Create a New App
Head to the [Meta for Developers](https://developers.facebook.com/apps/) portal and click the green **Create App** button in the top right.

![Meta Developer Portal - Create App](/images/providers/instagram/step-1.png)

### Step 1.1: Enter App Details
Give your app a name (e.g., "Agent Elephant"), enter your contact email, and click **Create App**.

![Meta Developer Portal - App Details](/images/providers/instagram/step-1.1.png)

---

### Step 2: Select Use Case
Meta offers many app templates. To secure the correct posting permissions:

1. Under the **Filter by** menu on the left side, click **All (19)** to see all available use cases.

![Select Use Case - All 19](/images/providers/instagram/step-2.png)

2. Scroll through the list and select **Manage messaging & content on Instagram**.

![Select Use Case - Manage Messaging and Content on Instagram](/images/providers/instagram/step-2.1.png)

3. Click the blue **Next** button.

![Select Use Case - Clicking Next](/images/providers/instagram/step-2.2.png)

4. On the next screen, confirm by clicking **Next** again.

![Confirm Use Case](/images/providers/instagram/step-2.3.png)

5. Click **Create App** to finalize.

![Create App Confirmation](/images/providers/instagram/step-2.4.png)

---

## Part 2: Configuring the App

Once your app is created, you will be dropped into the App Dashboard.

### Step 3: Set up the Redirect URI
To ensure a secure login loop, you need to whitelist Agent Elephant's callback address.

1. In the left sidebar, click on **API setup with Instagram login**.

![API Setup with Instagram Login Sidebar](/images/providers/instagram/Configuring-theApp-1.png)

2. Scroll down until you find the **Valid OAuth Redirect URIs** section and paste in your Agent Elephant redirect URI.

![Adding Redirect URI](/images/providers/instagram/Configuring-theApp-1.1.png)

> **Your Redirect URI:**
> - Production: `https://your-domain.com/api/settings/social/callback/instagram`
> - Local development: `http://localhost:3000/api/settings/social/callback/instagram`

3. Click **Save Changes**.

---

### Step 4: Copy Your Credentials

1. On the same page, scroll up to the top section where you can see **Instagram app ID** and **Instagram app secret**.

![App ID and App Secret location](/images/providers/instagram/Configuring-theApp-2.png)

2. Copy the **App ID**.

3. Click **Show** next to the App Secret, then copy it.

![Copy App Secret](/images/providers/instagram/Configuring-theApp-2.1.png)

4. Also make a note of the **Instagram app secret** shown on this same screen.

![Instagram App Secret Revealed](/images/providers/instagram/Configuring-theApp2.2.png)

---

## Part 3: Connecting in Agent Elephant

Now that you have your credentials, you can connect your account!

1. Go back to Agent Elephant's **Admin Settings**.
2. Click the **Manage Custom Apps** button.
3. Set the **Platform** dropdown to **Instagram**.
4. Paste in the **App ID** and **App Secret** you just copied from Meta.
5. Click **Save Credentials**.
6. Finally, click the **Connect** button under the Instagram integration, select your new credential profile, and follow the permissions prompts.

![Final Step - Connect Instagram in Agent Elephant](/images/providers/instagram/final-step.png)

Instagram is now connected! 🎉
